Stranger Things Seasons

Stranger Things Season 1

The disappearance of Will Byers and the emergence of Eleven, a young girl with psychokinetic abilities, sets off a chain of supernatural events in Hawkins.

Stranger Things Season 2

Will struggles with visions of the Upside Down while a new threat, the Mind Flayer, looms over Hawkins as Eleven discovers more about her past.

Stranger Things Season 3

Summer of 1985 brings new dangers as the Mind Flayer returns, threatening our heroes while they navigate teenage romance and a mysterious Russian facility.

Stranger Things Season 4

The gang faces their greatest challenge yet as a new supernatural threat emerges, forcing them to confront the horrors of the Upside Down once again.
